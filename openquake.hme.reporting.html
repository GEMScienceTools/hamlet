openquake.hme.reporting.reporting module¶

Functionality for writing reports demonstrating the results of the Hamlet testing.

openquake.hme.reporting.reporting.generate_basic_report(cfg: dict, results: dict, bin_gdf: Optional[geopandas.geodataframe.GeoDataFrame] = None, eq_gdf: Optional[geopandas.geodataframe.GeoDataFrame] = None) → None¶

Generates an HTML report with the results of the various evaluations or tests performed by Hamlet.

Parameters

  • cfg (dict) – Configuration from parsed yaml file.

  • results (dict) – Results of the model evaluations. Should be auto-generated by Hamlet.

  • bin_gdf (geopandas.GeoDataFrame) – All of the SpacemagBin s used in the evaluations.

  • eq_gdf (geopandas.GeoDataFrame) – Observed earthquakes used in the evaluations.
